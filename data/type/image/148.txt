
bayes-whales
Image classifier for finding quality ocean images in the NOAA Whales Aerial dataset. Uses a deep convolution network for binary classification. Submission for Bayes Hack 2016.
Play with a demo on a pretrained net. (accuracy: 92.67%)

Technologies Used

Caffe
pavlov/nin-imagenet
