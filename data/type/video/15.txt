
Video-Game-Sales-Analysis
The objective of this project is to perform Sales Analysis for Video Games Dataset in RShiny and to find different insights by performing visualizations with respect to year, publishers, global sales and Genre.
Dataset

Rows - 10100
Columns - 11

Dashboard
Overview


 It shows overall analysis of Video Game Sales from year 2007-2017 showing best and worst seller,publisher & genre.

Publisher Analysis


 It displays top selling publishers wrt copies sold(in millions)

Analysis from year 2007-2017


 It displays overall game sales trend from year 2007-2017 wrt genre.
