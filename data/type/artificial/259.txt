 opinion mining in costumer reviews for mcdonalds restaurants natural language processing or nlp as branch of artificial intelligence ai has found many important applications in human daily life in particular it has improved ways that computers and humans interact in this report we investigate some connections between nlp and business by analyzing dataset of 1525 negative reviews given by customers of low rated mcdonald fast food restaurants in the united states the reviews are labeled by one or several labels from eight categories we build model to classify each review to the most appropriate category at the end we evaluate our model performance by considering its accuracy and confusion matrix
